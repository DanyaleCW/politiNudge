PolitiNudge

Don't let COVID-19 impact your involvement with social justice movements that matter to you.

Mobilize online using this extension, which will remind you and direct you to the most highly effective online resources for change. This extension will swap the images on the NYTimes or Washington Post front page to politically themed images in support of causes of equality. Clicking on one of the swapped images will take you to the corresponding web page. Tired of politics? Try our cuddle mode.
